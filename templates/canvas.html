In this hands-on workshop, you'll learn the basics of creating your own personalized fragrant bag to take home. Choose from a variety of scents and colors to design the perfect aromatic accessory.

How to Make Fragrant Bags: You'll begin by selecting the fabric and fragrance materials. Then, you’ll cut and sew the fabric, fill the bag with your chosen scents, and finish it off with decorative elements. Finally, you’ll have a beautifully crafted fragrant bag that reflects your unique style!

Duration: 2 hours | Materials included | No prior experience required

**Time**: From 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM

**Location**: Ngũ Hành Sơn District, Đà Nẵng city
